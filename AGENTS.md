# AGENTS.md

## Public documentation rules

- Keep every document product-facing, concise, and safe for public release.
- Never use em dash or en dash characters. Use commas, parentheses, colons, semicolons, or separate sentences instead.
- Do not publish production code, credentials, private infrastructure details, provider configuration, internal URLs, or proprietary operational context.
- Keep pseudocode non-executable and limited to public product behavior.
- Use Vox domain language consistently, especially for AI Public Draft Note, Community Approved, Established, Bridging Consensus, and Governed Note.
- Scan every Markdown file for forbidden dash characters before commit.
